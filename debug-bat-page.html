Porsche 991 GT3

The 991 GT3 debuted in 2013 with the seven-speed PDK becoming the only transmission available. For 2018, the 991.2 reintroduced the six-speed manual to the lineup as well as a wingless GT3 Touring variant and a GT3 RS with an available Weissach Package like the GT2 RS.
